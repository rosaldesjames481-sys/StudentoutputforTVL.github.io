About the Website

This website was developed as a web-based system prototype intended to showcase selected student outputs from the Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL) strand of Alimodian National Comprehensive High School.

The platform serves as a digital repository and promotional tool that highlights students’ competencies, creativity, and hands-on skills acquired through outcome-based education.

This project was created by TVL ICT students as part of an academic and research requirement aligned with Information and Communications Technology specialization.
